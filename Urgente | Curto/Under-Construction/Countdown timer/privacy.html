REGISTRATION AND SELECTION PRIVACY POLICY (B2B & B2C)

1. SCOPE

Nanosegundo values the privacy of personal and corporate data. This Policy applies to Individuals and Legal Entities (Freelancers/Companies), ensuring compliance with POPIA (South Africa), Law No. 09/2024 (Mozambique), and Laws 001-2021 / 010-2004 (Burkina Faso).

2. DATA COLLECTED (Individuals & Entities)

We collect only what is strictly necessary:

  • 1. Individuals: Full Name, ID (BI/CNIB/Smart ID), Tax ID (NUIT/NIF), CV, and bank details.
  • 2. Companies (PJ): Corporate Name, Tax ID (NIF/SARS), Articles of Incorporation, Tax Clearance Certificates (INSS/DGI), and institutional bank details.

3. PURPOSES: The processing of data aims for:

  • (a) Qualification: Technical and legal assessment of the partner/candidate;
  • (b) Contract: Formalization of the service provision;
  • (c) Legal: Compliance with local tax and regulatory obligations.

4. SECURITY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

We treat trade secrets and personal data with strict encryption and access controls, preventing unauthorized exposure to third parties.

5. RETENTION AND RIGHTS

Data is kept for the duration of the contract or for 1 year in our talent/partner database. Access, correction, or deletion requests must be sent to: info@nanosegundo.tech
